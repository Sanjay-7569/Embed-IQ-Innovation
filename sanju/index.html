About Company

Embed IQ Innovation Private Limited specializes in multilayer PCB design optimized for performance and manufacturability, embedded systems development, and rapid prototyping & testing for electronic products. We offer custom solutions for IoT and smart devices, firmware/microcontroller programming, and enclosure & industrial design. Our team brings your ideas to life with functional prototypes and production-ready documentation, supporting you from concept to market.

  • Embedded systems firmware & microcontroller development
  • Rapid prototyping & validation
  • IoT & Smart Devices for consumer, industrial, and healthcare
  • Plastic and sheet metal casing design
  • Manufacturing support & BOM optimization
